# Product Requirements Document (PRD)

# Project Name

UniVerse

---

# Product Vision

UniVerse is an AI-powered student super app that helps college students connect with people, access campus resources, and solve everyday campus problems through a single platform.

Instead of searching through WhatsApp groups, Instagram pages, and university websites, students can use one application for everything they need.

---

# Problem Statement

College students often depend on scattered platforms for information and communication.

Examples include:

- Lost & Found through WhatsApp groups
- Finding roommates
- Finding teammates for hackathons and competitions
- Campus announcements
- Internship opportunities
- Club information
- Academic guidance

This creates unnecessary confusion, delays, and missed opportunities.

---
