# 🌌 UniVerse

> One platform for everything a college student needs.

---

## 📖 About

UniVerse is an AI-powered student super app designed to simplify campus life by bringing together essential student services into one platform.

Instead of relying on multiple WhatsApp groups, websites, and social media pages, students can use UniVerse to connect with people, discover opportunities, and access important campus resources from a single place.

The long-term vision is to make UniVerse a platform that can be adopted by universities across India.
